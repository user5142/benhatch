About

I am a writer, internet maker and above all else, family man. The best things in life I have ever done were marrying my wife and becoming a dad.

I grew up in a small town in Massachusetts. I studied business at the University of Vermont, then moved back to Massachusetts and have lived here ever since. I will probably be a New Englander for life. I considered a move to the West Coast to chase lofty dreams in my twenties, but it never panned out, which I am grateful for. I have fallen further in love with the farms, mountains, forests, rivers, disc golf courses and seasons of my home turf as I have gotten older.

I have had many passions and interests over the years, but one constant is that I have always been obsessed with creating. As a young boy, I used to draw freehand copies of Mickey Mouse, Donald Duck and other Disney characters. In my teen years, I fell in love with freestyle skiing, built terrain park features in my backyard and produced skiing videos with my friends. In my twenties, I made short films, wrote movie scripts, became a standup comic and co-founded a CBD business. These days, I am building MrMarket.

The act of creating brings me lots of fulfillment. I love making things that others can enjoy and benefit from. I also have a highly curious mind, and a strong appetite for learning. I have taught myself how to code, design, write, invest, produce videos, manage finances, start a business, think critically about the world and much more.

This website serves as the central hub for all of my creative and business endeavors. I hope to share hard-earned wisdom, useful products, interesting business lessons and high caliber dad jokes on here and other channels.

To learn more about what I am up to now, check out my now page.
