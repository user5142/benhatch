The Law of 100

Nov 11, 2024

I first came across The Law of 100 from this Noah Kagan tweet:

When starting something new, follow The Law of 100.

Put out 100 videos.
Record 100 podcasts.
Reach out to 100 potential customers.

Get your reps in and you will see results. pic.twitter.com/ZLS4S3t2SH

— Noah Kagan (@noahkagan) January 9, 2024

I love the simplicity and power of this law. It applies to starting something new, and involves doing the thing - writing articles, designing websites, making short films, whatever the thing is - 100 times and getting the reps in before worrying about optimization or results.

In thinking back on my prior business and creative endeavors, I would have been served well to follow The Law of 100 and give myself a fair shot at success. I feel there were several pursuits in my past that I could have turned into something if I had just stuck it out and focused on getting those first 100 reps in.

In the spirit of not repeating history, I have committed to putting The Law of 100 into action. I will be making 100 videos for my new YouTube channel.

As I publish each video, I will post them to the list below until I reach the 100 mark. I am excited to focus on the process, hone my skills and become a disciple of The Law of 100!
