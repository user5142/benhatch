Writing blog articles

I have been greatly enjoying a rekindling with the art of writing lately. Specifically, I have been working on blog articles for this website. I have always loved writing, and have written on and off in different forms since I was a young lad. I wrote a feature length movie script in my twenties titled “Cascade” about a zombie outbreak at a ski resort in Maine. I never showed it to anyone (which gives you an idea of its quality).

Recording YouTube videos

I have also been enjoying a rekindling with the creation of YouTube videos. I created my first YouTube channel in 2007 when I was 17 years old. The channel is still up, and I have no idea how to take it down (I haven’t actually tried). I have had a few channels over the years. With this new one, I am starting out very simple. I will be posting videos of me talking about some of the topics I have written about on my blog. The videos will expand on the topics, and likely include some cringe dad humor. I may also record some videos on topics I have not covered on the blog.

Settling into new job

I work full-time for a medical device company, and recently took on a new position. I was previously Customer Service Supervisor and am now working as a Business Analyst on the IT team. I am focused on coordinating projects, creating test protocols, designing reporting dashboards and a number of other initiatives for our ERP system. Things have already been very busy, and I am still transitioning out of my previous role, but I am very excited about this new direction and all the growth and learning that will come with it.
