Indie Maker Icons

Icon Design

Indie Maker Icons is an indie maker themed icon set that I designed in 2022.

This was my very first attempt at designing an icon set. I designed the icons in Adobe Illustrator and leaned heavily on Dima Groshev's Medium article, Design principles for creating the perfect icon set, to create a cohesive and effective set.
