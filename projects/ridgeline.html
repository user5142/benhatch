Ridgeline

Website Design

UI Design

Wireframes

Figma

Ridgeline is an ecommerce product page I designed as part of Codecademy’s Introduction to UI/UX Design Course.

The project involved first desiging two product page wireframe options for a fictitious outdoor apparel brand called Ridgeline, then turning one of the designs into a high-fidelity prototype.

I designed both the wireframes and the final prototype in Figma.

To create a more realistic look and feel for the project, I added a custom Ridgeline logo, which I designed in Adobe Illustrator.

You can check out the final Ridgeline prototype and view the Figma source file.
